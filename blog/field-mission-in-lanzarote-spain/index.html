Field mission in Lanzarote (Spain)

Giovanni Beltrame

2019-03-13 23:17

Our field mission with the European Space Agency for the PANGAEA-X experimental campaign was a success! We deployed 6 flying robots, controlled with a single tablet by one of the European astronauts.

Our experiments were also featured on the science show Tomorrow Today - The Science Magazine. You can see the full reportage here.
